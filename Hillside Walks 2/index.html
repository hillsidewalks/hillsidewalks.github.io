About Us

Your pet, our passion


At Hillside Walks, we are dedicated to providing exceptional group dog walking services in the beautiful surroundings of Revelstoke. I am a passionate and experienced dog walker who ensures that every outing is safe, enjoyable, and enriching for your fur babies. We believe in fostering a sense of community and adventure, giving dogs the exercise, socialization, and stimulation they need to thrive. Join us and let your dogs explore the great outdoors with their new pack!
